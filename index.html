  • Section — 01
  • CSF — Overview

CSF is a University of the Arts Research Centre based at London College of Fashion. Our work is a key aspect of London College of Fashion’s commitment to Better Lives using fashion to drive change, build a sustainable future and improve the way we live.

  • Section — 02
  • Brand Attributes

Fashion shapes and reflects society and communities, their culture and diversity, it is both personal and ubiquitous, an every day phenomenon. Centre for Sustainable Fashion (CSF) was devised to question and challenge a mainly reactionary fashion culture, one which reflects and re-enforces patterns of excessive consumption and disconnection.
